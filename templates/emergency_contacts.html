Important Notes

  • 112 is the universal emergency number that works from mobile phones even without airtime or signal
  • 10111 is the national police emergency number for all serious crimes
  • 10177 connects you to both ambulance and fire services
  • Save these emergency numbers in your phone contacts for quick access
  • Teach children these important emergency numbers
  • In case of emergency, stay calm and clearly state your location and the nature of the emergency
